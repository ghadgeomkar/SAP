SAP Business One

What is SAP Business One?

A single, affordable solution to manage your entire small business – from accounting and financials, purchasing, inventory, sales and customer relationships to reporting and analytics.

Gain greater control over your business or subsidiary with SAP Business One. Streamline key processes, gain greater insight into your business, and make decisions based on real-time information – so you can drive profitable growth.

  • On-premise or cloud deployment
  • Integrated business intelligence
  • Integration with the SAP HANA platform
  • Quick deployment

SAP Business One Web Client

SAP Business One, Web client brings you the best of all worlds. Based on SAP Fiori design principles, it encapsulates the core processes and business logic of the SAP Business One solution.

SAP Business One Web Client

Create, process, and update marketing documents and master data, including approval processes. With SAP Business One, Web client. you can create and track activities and benefit from sophisticated analytical charting capabilities and predefined analytics content. In addition, the client can be deployed either in the cloud or on-premise, including side-by-side use.

SAP Business One Sales App

Empower your entire sales organization to manage information on the move anytime, anywhere, and on any mobile device.

SAP Business One Service App

SAP Business One Service mobile app for iOS and Android helps your service team manage their workload anywhere. With the app, they can view customer service records, ticket information, check stock, place an order and capture a customer signature, for more effective customer service management on the move.

Frequently asked questions about SAP Business One

SAP Business One is an affordable solution to manage small and midsize companies: from accounting and financials, purchasing, inventory, sales and customer relationships (CRM) to reporting and analytics.

SAP Business One is designed for small and midsize business (SMB) enterprises, while SAP S/4HANA Cloud and SAP ERP are designed for organizations of all sizes.

Because every business is different, SAP Business One is designed with flexibility in mind. Whether it’s deployed on-premise or in the cloud, you can access SAP Business One at anytime, anywhere using any mobile device. As your business grows, you can customize and extend SAP Business One to meet your evolving needs.
